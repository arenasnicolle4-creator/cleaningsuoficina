Who We Are

More than a cleaning company

Cleaning Su Oficina is the commercial division of the Cleaning Su Casa family — Anchorage’s trusted cleaning service since 2020. We bring the same reliability, attention to detail, and genuine care that Anchorage homes have counted on for years, now scaled for the demands of commercial spaces.

From a small law office to a full hotel property, we work around your schedule, adapt to your facility’s specific needs, and deliver consistent results every single visit.

Experienced, background-checked cleaners
Professional-grade equipment and products
Tailored cleaning plans for every facility
After-hours and weekend scheduling available
Locally owned and operated in Anchorage
